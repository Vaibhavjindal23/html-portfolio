The Best Movies According to Vaibhav

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bhool Bhulaiya

This is my favourite movie. I love the funny punches used in the movie. The main actor in the movie is 'Akshay Kumar' who plays the role of a popular pyschiatrist.

Lukka Chuppi

Really cool and funny movie. The starcast includes 'Kartik Aryan', 'Kriti Sanon' and 'Pankaj Tripathi'. This movie throws light on the very controversial subject- 'Live-In Relationships in India'.

Golmaal

Super beautiful film. Really artistic. The humour is next level in this film.
